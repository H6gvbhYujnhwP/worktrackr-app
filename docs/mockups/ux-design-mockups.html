5. Order form — one-off job

Starts blank. Build it by hand, or pull one or several IdoYourQuotes quotes — each pulled line brings its description, sell, buy-in cost and profit straight from that quote. IDYQ is the source of truth, so to change a margin the salesperson edits the quote in IDYQ and WorkTrackr re-pulls (read-only here). Manually-added lines are costed in WorkTrackr. Either way, totals auto-calculate and feed the commission & bonus screen.
